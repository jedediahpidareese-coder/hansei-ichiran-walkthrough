15  The variables

The dataset has 107 columns organized into nine logical families. This page is a reference; you don’t need to memorize it.

15.1 The variable families at a glance

Family What it captures # of columns Cite as
Identifiers Domain canonical name, HI old-kanji name, romanization 5 Project crossref (internal)
Kokudaka (the IV) The various revenue assessments — sōdaka, omotedaka, naidaka, godaka, gendaka 14 Hansei Ichiran (1870)
Population & demographics Total population, household counts, status-class composition 22 HI 1870 + Meiji Shiyō / Ōkuma / Tōkei Shūshi (primary sources)
Military capacity Troop counts, warship counts, artillery, doctrine 22 HI 1870 vol. 2 (sparse — see below)
Fiscal Tax receipts (rice + cash), miscellaneous tax 1 HI 1870
Ravina A1 ratios Five published per-capita ratios from Ravina (1999) Appendix A1 5 Ravina (1999)
HI-derived & primary-derived Project-computed analogues of the Ravina ratios from our own data 16 Derived
Diagnostics Confidence flags, completeness scores, divergence metrics 5 Internal
Classification (paper controls) Domain category (親藩/譜代/外様), Boshin alignment, kazoku rank 14 Multiple — see DV chapters

15.2 The most important columns for the paper

15.2.1 IV — Kokudaka (column kokudaka_iv)

The composite kokudaka — the maximum of the available HI fields (草高, 合高, 表高) plus the Ōkuma / Meiji-Shiyō 表高 fallback — per domain.

  • Coverage: 276/287
  • Range: ~2,000 to ~1,400,000 koku
  • Why composite: HI reports different fields for different domains. Saga uses 貮口合 (= 合高); Chōshū uses 草高; some small domains only have 表高. The composite picks the most-comprehensive figure available.
  • Citation: a sibling source column records which field was actually used for each domain

15.2.2 DV — 1884 kazoku rank (column kazoku_rank_actual)

The peerage rank awarded in 1884, one of 5 tiers.

  • Coverage: 284/287 (3 documented exclusions)
  • Distribution: 3 prince, 15 marquess, 35 count, 222 viscount, 9 baron
  • Citation chain: 浅見雅男『華族誕生』(1994) + 官報 第307号 (1884) + 華族大鑑刊行会編 (1990) for princes
  • See the Kazoku Rank chapter for the full story.

15.2.3 Treatment — Boshin alignment (column boshin_alignment_group)

Each domain’s side in the Boshin War.

  • Coverage: 287/287
  • Values: anti-bakufu / pro-restoration, pro-bakufu, neutral
  • Distribution: 236 anti-bakufu, 41 pro-bakufu, 10 neutral
  • Citation chain: Esposito (2020) + 工藤威 (2002) + Beasley (1972). See the Boshin Alignment chapter.

15.2.4 Paper-relevant — Genmai-rule deviations (column kazoku_rank_deviation_genmai)

The 19 daimyo families whose actual 1884 rank diverged from the mechanical prediction.

  • Coverage: 19/287 (most domains land where the rule predicts)
  • Why this column exists: Asami (1994) argues the 1884 threshold operated on 現米 (genmai) — post-Meiji-2 tax-reported rice flow — not 草高/表高. Applying the genmai rule, 19 cases show real deviations. These are the paper’s core sample.
  • See The 19 Deviations chapter.

15.3 A note on military data sparsity

The military columns are interesting but sparse:

Gap state Count What it means
COMPLETE 8 Full troop/warship/artillery roster
PARTIAL 173 Some military data, some missing
HI_OMITTED 6 Source explicitly says 書ナラス (“not recorded”)
HI_OMITTED_PARTIAL 14 Mixed — some military fields recorded, some 書ナラス
NO_MILITARY_DATA 85 Vol. 1-only domains — no T8/T9 entry expected (small fiefs without standing forces)
SOURCE_GAP 1 Saga — wholly absent from T7-T9

For the paper’s actual military analysis, the focal-4 supplements (Satsuma, Chōshū, Tosa, Saga) draw from published scholarly sources (Esposito 2020, Craig 1961, Roberts 1998, 藤野保 2010) rather than from HI’s sparse military columns alone.

15.4 How to find anything

The single-source-of-truth is outputs/hansei_ichiran_reocr/data_provenance.md — every column has its source, citation guidance, coverage %, and known caveats. The next chapters tell the story; this file is the lookup.

Reference: data_provenance.md is the per-column technical reference. Per-row citations are stored in adjacent *_source columns where applicable (e.g., kazoku_rank_source, boshin_alignment_source, domain_category_source).
